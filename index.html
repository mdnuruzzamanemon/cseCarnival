About CSE Carnival

“ CSE Carnival 2024 is a three-day celebration of technology, innovation, and creativity, taking place at Green University of Bangladesh. This event promises a unique blend of interactive seminars, competitions, workshops, and cultural programs, organized by the Department of CSE in collaboration with the Green University Computer Club (GUCC), IEEE Student Branch GUB, IEEE Computer Society Student Branch Chapter GUB, and BASIS Students’ Forum GUB Chapter. “

Join us for an exciting experience that brings together students, professionals, and industry experts to inspire, challenge, and influence the future generation of innovators.
